Roasted Red Potatoes

Roasted red potatoes are a delicious side dish that pairs well with a variety of main courses. They're easy to make and require minimal ingredients.
To make roasted red potatoes, start by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). Wash and scrub the potatoes, then cut them into bite-sized pieces. Toss the potatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, and any other seasonings you like. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast for 30-40 minutes, or until they're golden brown and crispy on the outside.
Serve the roasted red potatoes hot as a side dish with your favorite meal. They're sure to be a hit with your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds red potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
